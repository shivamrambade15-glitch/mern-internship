Popular Destinations

A few of the places our travelers return to again and again.

Kyoto, Japan

Cultural

Wooden machiya houses, hidden tea rooms and temples tucked into the hills — perfect for slow explorers.

Ideal stay: 6–8 days Best months: Mar–May
Tea houses Temples City walks

Amalfi Coast, Italy

Coastal

Lemon groves, cliffside villages and slow evenings on the water — a classic summer escape.

Ideal stay: 4–7 days Best months: May–Sep
Sea views Boats Seafood

Chiang Mai, Thailand

Laid-back

Markets, mountains and cozy cafés — perfect for remote work or a gentle introduction to Southeast Asia.

Ideal stay: 2–4 weeks Best months: Nov–Feb
Remote work Temples Street food

Lisbon, Portugal

City + Coast

Trams, tiled streets and surf breaks just outside the city — ideal for a long weekend or a month.

Ideal stay: 5–10 days Best months: Mar–Jun
Cafés Ocean Day trips

Patagonia, Chile & Argentina

Adventure

Glacier-fed lakes, endless trails and big skies — for hikers who love dramatic landscapes.

Ideal stay: 10–14 days Best months: Oct–Mar
Trekking Wildlife Camping

Greek Islands

Island-hopping
